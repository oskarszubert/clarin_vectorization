Trzy kocie ruchy Google-Fu i oto na blogu d0x.com fragment szczegółowego wyjaśnienia od człowieka z Google:  When a keyword-insertion ad appears on a search result page, the AdWords system inserts the keyword that triggered the ad into the ad text. If the keyword is too long, and would cause the ad text to exceed our character limits, the ad’s default text would be used instead. In rare cases, the system may insert a keyword that causes the ad to exceed the character limit by one or two characters. There’s no guaranteed way to exceed the character limit, so we don’t recommend that you tailor your ad text to attempt this. It would likely make keyword insertion less effective for you, since the AdWords system will almost always use your default text in place of a too-long keyword.
