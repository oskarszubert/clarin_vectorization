Dziś będzie o dziwnym świecie Mikołaja Drawskiego - fotografa nieistniejących światów; a może raczej nieoczywistych? Bo co tam tak naprawdę jest? Czy ciało, umysł i natura są jednością?﻿ Czy pozory przeżerają/pożerają świat? Czy zabawki są niewinne? Czy feeria barw rzeczywiście rozświetla krainę wiecznego szczęścia? Czy uśmiech na pewno pokazuje radość? --- Today I'm not going to say much; the today's post is like "A picture is worth a thousand words". Today you'll visit the strange word of Mikołaj Drawski - a photographer of non-existent worlds, or maybe worlds not so obvious? What else is there? Are body, mind and nature really united? Is the world devoured / eroded by appearances? Are toys innocent? Does the color spree in fact light up the land of eternal happiness? Ukłony (inne niż zwykle), Dagmara --- The art photography comes out of galleries and enters homes where it makes interiors really different; especially the pictures which are﻿ unique, the ones which are art. Does it have to be joyful? I don't know. I guess it depends on expectations towards it - is it to be just a pretty thing? or is it to be a kind of memento? Is it meant to be a nice reproduction or should it bring you to the artist's world? Like the world above where Mexican wrestlers undermine the spectator's certainty of what's around. The answer is yours and yours only. Best regards (not so obvious), Dagmara
