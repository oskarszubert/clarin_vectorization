I’m coming back though happily and tasty, bringing back an old post which I feel has to be updated. Do you remember the family of Toasts and Waffles? This time the big return on the FORelements is Oriental and comic, or – Ladies and Gentlemen – we’re serving Manga straight from a plate. More precisely – toasts with Manga. You can add a mango (which I love!!!) but the main character here is the Japanese breakfast art of – let me put it this way – a graphic and a baker, Hittomii.﻿﻿ Let’s be honest – you have to get up really early, at the break of dawn to make a breakfast like that because it’s rather time-consuming and demands going into the Zen state of mind. Hittomii first draws a pattern on a piece of foil, cuts out its lines, then overlays a slightly baked toast with such stencil and besprinkles it with cacao powder. Colorful pieces are made the same way, besprinkled with natural ingredients, for example with green tea powder. The artist doesn’t want to tell how to make very light parts of the image but some people say and suspect that she uses white chocolate.
