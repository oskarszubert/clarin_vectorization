Mini - cupcakes with lemon curd, cream and advocaat Ingredients for about 26 mini - cupcakes:                    120 g butter                   110 g caster sugar with vanilla seeds 2 large eggs                   200 g plain flour 2 tsp baking powder                   3 Tbs advocaat (45 ml) Also:                    250 ml whipping cream 1 Tbs icing sugar                   3/4 cup lemon curd All ingredients should be at room temperature. Sift flour with baking powder, set aside.           Place butter and sugar in a mixing bowl and beat until pale and fluffy. Add eggs, one by one, mixing until smooth after each addition. Add sifted flour and baking powder, mix with spatula. Add advocaat and mix until just blended.           Spoon the mixture into mini - muffin tin lined with muffin cases.           Bake at 80 - 190ºC for about 15 minutes or until cooked when tested with skewer. Place on a wire rack to cool.             Whip the cream until stiff, adding 1 tsp icing sugar at the end of whipping. Using a pastry bag with a plain tip pipe whipped cream onto a cupcake, in a shape of a circle. Pipe a lemon curd into the middle.  Enjoy!
