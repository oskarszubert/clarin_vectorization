NEVADA of United States16.03.2011 | kategoria: English    Nevada is a state placed in a west part of Unated States. Its surface are mostly hills, plateaus, deserts and mountains. Nevada is cut by a mountain chain called Sierra Nevada – the Snow Mountains. Thats why this state is called that way – Nevada. The biggest cities in Nevada are Las Vegas, Reno and Henderson. Las Vegas is one of the most important entertainment and recreational touristic places in USA. It’s on the Mojave desert and its the biggest city founded in XX century (1905 year).  It’s known as a gambling centre and many other atractions. Because of many neons Las Vegas has become a most lighted place visible from the space. There is a synthetic Lake Mead National Recreation Area right above the dam. Below the dam the Colorado river creates one of the world greatest miracles – The Great Canyon. Colorado River - Hover Dam The Joshua Tree West from Las Vegas is a NTS (Nevada Test Site) – the nuclear range, strategic aircraft base Nellis AFB and the famous Area 51 – super secret military base. There is also about 1000 craters created after underground nuclear blasts.  Its easy to find there a very interesting plant – The Joshua Tree. Furher to the west is a very unusual place – Zabriskie Point. Its a viewing point of the National Park of Death Valley, well known because there’s interesting shapes of rocks there. Death Valley is a place with the lowest placed depression in the world – 86 meters below the sea level. Its one of the most warm and dry places in the world – temperature reaches over 56.7 °C (134°F) degree.  Million years ago there was placed an Ocean, but now all thats left after the ocean are loads of salt. Death Valley lies partially in the Nevada states on the Mojave desert.
