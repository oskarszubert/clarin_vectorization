Jessy Mercedes Last Friday evening was a time of prestigous, some kind of glamorous gala, closing a well-known Poznań Art Fashion Festival in Stary Browar. During two weeks of this unimaginable festivity, some chosen artists had a unique chance to learn a little bit of a professional photography, how to draw or how to make a movie and last but not least, how to design some fabulous garment and bijouterie what obviously would be a fantastic vista for further developing their skills and talents. Every single day during this event you could join lectures called Open University AFF, which were lectured by inspiring human beings such as Nicholas Kirkwood, Piotr Zachara, Joanna Bojańczyk or Mikołaj Komar. What's more, at this beautifuly ending festival night we got a chance to admire an outstanding work results of those who joined our lectures and we could see a catwalk show of Krzystof Stróżyna and Hector Karger duo, and all this was for upcoming spring/summer 2012. W piątek wieczorem odbyła się uroczysta gala zamykająca kultowy poznański Art Fashion Festival w Straym Browarze. Podczas dwóch tygodni festiwalu wybrani artyści uczyli się na zamkniętych warsztatach modowej fotografii, rysunku, czy filmu oraz projektowania ubrań i biżuterii. Codziennie można było odwiedzić wykłady Open Univeristy AFF, które prowadzili tak inspirujący ludzie jak Nicholas Kirkwood, Piotr Zachara, Joanna Bojańczyk, czy Mikołaj Komar. A na gali zamykającej ten festiwal, podziwialiśmy efekty pracy warsztatowiczów i obejrzeliśmy pokaz mody Krystof Strozyna oraz duetu Hector Karger na sezon wiosna/lato 2012.
