Jessy Mercedes Hektor Świtalski and Rober Karger are the London based duo behind the HECTOR   KARGER label. Hektor studied MA Fashion womenswear at the London College of Fashion and was also offered a place at the legendary Central Saint Martins College. Robert Karger was born in Poland, but grew up in Australia. Currently, while living between London and Poland where Robert is graduating in stage design at the Academy of Fine Arts in Poznań, he is realising his professional vision with each women- subject he creates for.  Hector Karger's dynamic and modern-day woman is attracted to clothes and wears style with expression. The designs carry a refreshin mind-set, changing the way a woman can want to see herself. The duo's direction is the so-called modern feminity. They are highly valued for a bold style full of contrasts and clear-cuts. It's full-blooded with body. No youth, prudery, cheek and devasting elegance make the signature in their collections. The first boutique is loccated at the presitgious Stary Browar Business and Art Centre in Poznań in Poland. I really love effects of joint cooperation with the designers and the great photographer Krzysztof Adamek.
