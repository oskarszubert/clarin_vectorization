Jessy Mercedes Sometimes I am more elegant, at times more rock, but I always stay chic. I love how all the details fit together and here the studs on my favourite biker jacket, bag and shoes are explicitly created for themselves. Have you seen Isabel Marant's proposal for this season? Tomboy models wearing light pink pants? Although I chose to wear long trousers in a more intensive coral shade - this show inspired me today so much!
