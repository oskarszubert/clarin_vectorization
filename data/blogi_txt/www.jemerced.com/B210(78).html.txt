Jessy Mercedes A lot of you are asking me what I am wearing at a party, on a girl's night out or when I'm just going to a pub with my best friends. I love to match something shiny with a lot of comfortable elements from my wardrobe. I am almost never wearing pants, I'm a big fan of leggins and skirt. I'm trying to pack all my stuff that I always need to a clutch or an another smaller bag, then I am usually wearing. Enjoy this pics, love!
