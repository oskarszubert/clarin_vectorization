Jessy Mercedes It is not that often to see me wearing casual stylizations and this is because of that I rarely put something that is not elegant or glamour. It was a gusty day, breezy, cooling air, so I had to slip into something warm and gauzy. It seems to me that those pictures that were taken with the mobile phone are quite swell, so I had to share it with you.  P.S. Take a close look at those little shiny crystals around shoulder stitch - it is a trend that was brought to the light by Prada that used it on its coats from the spring/summer 2012 collection. Those petit crystals around the shoulder line can be found in many chain shops nowadays and what's interesting - in the fall colelctions.
