A view like from Big Day, thus it necessarily I had use it as a background for one of my shootings. Today, I would like to show you 3years trends: first of them is revealing of your belly, or I would rather say ‘the zone’ over your navel but still under your bra and ribs. This season Prada and Zuo Corp. stand for this kind of look. Another trend is a street trend. That is camo style! Army jackets have been plugged by some trendsetters on the streets of big cities for like couple of last seasons, though they usually used khaki colour. This season it’s a quintessential breakthrough and un pas for khaki that is being used by all women, it’s like they all went berserk! The last trend speaks of the party one, we cease to choose boring black rubber boots, rather going for some clean-cut, kind of expressive ones! Baby pink Hunters stole my heart and you can easily see me wearing them this whole summer while the weather is kind of rainy with some somber clouds.  Kolejny post z Trójmiasta. Ten sezon to przełom i krok do przodu khaki zaczyna pokrywać deseń wojskowy, na punkcie, którego oszalały wszystkie kobiety! Ostatni trend to ten festiwalowy, przestajemy wybierać nudne czarne kalosze, stawiamy na te najbardziej wyraziste!
