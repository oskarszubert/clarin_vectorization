Jessy Mercedes I want fur! I want to wear it on the head, back, arms or feet. I want to look like a Russian woman, still wearing romantic frills and rock accessories.  I have needed a skirt like this for a long time... I chose colours, which I never wear, but they remind me about our beautiful polish autumn, when you can see gold, reddish and brown leaves everywhere! I had to sew this skirt all weekend, but the results are satisfactory.   ﻿Pragnę, pragnę futer! Na głowie, plecach, ramionach, czy stopach.
