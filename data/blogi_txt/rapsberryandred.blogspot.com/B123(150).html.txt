Next spring/summer is going to be very interesting. Some of the designs amazed me (e.g. Valentino- these dresses are to die for!) but some- disappointed a little. Take Burberry Prorsum- my favourite brand ever. The latest show? LOVE the music, hate the neon clothes. The same is with Marc Jacobs. After the classical show, he brought back models looking super-weird with all these hairstyles and make-up. Some of you may say: "And this is called creativity". Well, I don't like it but hey, it's just my personal opinion ;) Wiosna i lato zapowiadają się bardzo ciekawie. Niektóre z kolekcji mnie zachwyciły (np.
