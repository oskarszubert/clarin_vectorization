Domyślił się, że padał deszcz.*     Alain Aslan Pink Pin Up, "Oui", February 1974 issue   Dear PINK's     I saw this drawing for the first time at the age of 8 or 9 – around 1977/78. It was in one of the two french men's mags I got from my uncle. In "Oui" I believe. They both featured Aslan's drawings – in the other one ("Lui") there was an image of a girl wearing hand woven cream fishnet dress - my granny Halinka doesn't wanted to believe that this was a drawing because the crotchet pattern was so neatly done :) I liked them both a lot but this one hounted me all my life. I was extremely happy to found it in new Aslan's album I bought two years ago in Brussels.
